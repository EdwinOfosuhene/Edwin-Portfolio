Gamez.

Innovation / Web design + Development

duration: September,2024 -

platform: Web

Collaborators: This is an independent project

Gamez is a curated blog website where I share honest, in-depth reviews of every PlayStation 5 game I’ve played over the years. From story-driven adventures to fast-paced shooters, I’ve documented my thoughts on gameplay, visuals, replay value, and overall experience—all from a player’s perspective. This project took me one month to complete, handling everything from design and layout to content writing and frontend development. Creating Gamez has been personally rewarding, as it’s given me a space to reflect on my gaming experiences, sharpen my writing, and continuously improve my frontend design skills through a project I genuinely care about. For me, it’s more than just a blog—it’s a creative outlet and a digital archive of the stories, challenges, and moments that stuck with me across different games. For fellow gamers, Gamez offers honest, relatable reviews from a player’s perspective, helping them discover new titles or revisit old favorites with fresh insights. It’s a space where shared passion for gaming meets thoughtful design, making it enjoyable to read, browse, and connect over the love of PlayStation 5 games.

Inspiration

As a lifelong gamer and designer, I’ve always found myself reflecting on the games I play—what made them memorable, what frustrated me, and how they made me feel. Over time, I realized I wanted a space to capture those thoughts in a structured, personal, and creative way. Gamez was born out of that desire: a place where I could combine my love for storytelling through games with my passion for building clean, user-focused digital experiences. I was inspired by how game review sites organize content but wanted to create something more personal, more visual, and more reflective of my own journey as both a player and a designer.
